my fourth year in an american suburb

i love taking photos in pittsburgh. i spent my coming of age here, living alone for the first time. every moment and picture i capture i felt like i owned. i conquered something about here and something about myself. at the same time, this slice of american suburbia has felt so foreign to me, as a child of immigrants. this space can never be truly mine. i am interested in photographing moments in a fleeting, casual way the same way i experience them, canonizing them in their small way, and it felt wrong to present them in a formalized photography setting. rather than curate combinations and framings, i felt that leaving it up to chance better reflects the way i experienced them. i have my favorites but i hope you enjoy the four you get - they were picked just for you. if you don't, you can always change what you get.
